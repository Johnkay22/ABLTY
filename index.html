The Research
THIS IS REAL SCIENCE
For over two decades, the U.S. government funded classified research into human psi abilities. The results were significant enough to run the program for 23 years. That research is now declassified — and the methodology is the foundation of ABLTY.
Declassified · CIA · 1995
Project STARGATE
A 23-year, $20 million U.S. government program at Stanford Research Institute investigating remote viewing. Declassified in 1995. Researchers Hal Puthoff and Russell Targ demonstrated statistically significant results across thousands of trials. Program operated under the Defense Intelligence Agency.
Peer-Reviewed · Journal of Scientific Exploration
Meta-Analysis of Remote Viewing Experiments
A comprehensive meta-analysis of 653 remote viewing trials conducted across multiple laboratories found an overall hit rate significantly above chance (p < 0.0001). Effect sizes were consistent across independent research groups. — Utts, J. (1996). An Assessment of the Evidence for Psychic Functioning.
Princeton University · PEAR Lab · 1979–2007
Princeton Engineering Anomalies Research
28 years of rigorous laboratory research at Princeton University examining human-machine interactions and remote perception. Published over 80 technical reports. The remote perception database includes over 650 formal trials demonstrating consistent anomalous cognition effects.
Peer-Reviewed · Psychological Bulletin · 1994
Ganzfeld ESP Studies
A meta-analysis of 11 independent Ganzfeld databases (2,549 sessions) found a hit rate of 33.2% against a 25% chance baseline — a statistically significant result replicated across independent laboratories. — Bem, D. & Honorton, C. Does psi exist?

The Protocols
FOUR TRAINING SYSTEMS
Each module is grounded in a specific research methodology. Your data is tracked over time. Patterns emerge. The signal gets clearer.
01
REMOTE VIEWING
Project Stargate Protocol
Receive a blind target reference number. Sketch your impressions before analysis. AI grades your session across 6 dimensions against the hidden target. Based directly on the CRV methodology developed at SRI International for the U.S. government.
02
PSI TRAINING
Zener · Presentiment · Photo Pair
Three protocols in one hub. The Zener card test (25 trials, 20% chance baseline). Presentiment timing — based on Dean Radin's research showing measurable responses to future stimuli before they occur. Photo Pair intuition — tap the card hiding a pleasant image, testing unconscious orientation toward emotionally positive future stimuli.
03
DREAM LAB
Lucid Dream Training
Reality Check Pager sends randomized notifications throughout your day, training the perceptual habit that triggers lucid dreaming. Based on cognitive induction research showing frequency and randomness of checks predicts lucidity onset.
04
ASTRAL PROJECTION
OBE Induction · Coming Soon
Out-of-body experience induction and exit protocols. Guided sessions built around the hypnagogic transition window — the threshold state between waking and sleep where OBE onset is most frequently reported.
